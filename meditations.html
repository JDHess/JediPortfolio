Yuli's Work

Jedi Breath-Mantra Meditations

Breathe along to Jedi Mantras. For a sense of calm & memorization for times when it's needed.

These meditations were originally made at a time when I was in the process of a spiritual transition and needed a way to cope with my anxiety and have a sense of faith despite losing a significant amount of beliefs I had been holding on to. These meditations were based on and are adaptations of a practice I had already known and had been using to help bring calm. This will not cure anxiety, but in many ways, though the practice has helped me, I found that personally, the language needed to be modified due to beliefs changing and what I needed to focus on.

Precautions: If you're participating in the breathing part of these meditations... if you have COPD or Moderate hyperinflation, you're welcome to listen but it's advised not to participate in the breathing section of these meditations. Please talk to a doctor if you have any health concerns regarding these meditations.
